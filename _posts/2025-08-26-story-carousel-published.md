I’ve published `story_carousel` on pub.dev — an Instagram-like stories carousel for Flutter.

**What’s inside**
- Auto-play with per-item durations
- Tap left/right, long-press to pause
- Optional controller (next, prev, pause, resume)
- Progress bar
